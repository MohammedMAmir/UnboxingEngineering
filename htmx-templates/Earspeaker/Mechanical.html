Structural Design and Vibration Analysis

Mechanical engineers are responsible for the structural design of the ear speaker, ensuring it is compact yet durable. They perform vibration analysis to design speaker components that can withstand the physical stress of sound production without degrading over time. Their work ensures the speaker's components are mechanically sound and can be integrated seamlessly into the iPhone's slim profile.

Thermal Management

Mechanical engineers also address the thermal management within the ear speaker, designing components that dissipate heat effectively during operation. This is crucial for maintaining the integrity of the speaker materials and electronic components, ensuring consistent performance even with extended use.
