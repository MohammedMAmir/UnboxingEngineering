Ethical Sourcing of Metals

Mineral engineers ensure that the metals used in the volume off switch, such as those in the contacts and springs, are ethically sourced. They focus on minimizing the environmental and social impacts of metal extraction and processing.

Recycling and Material Recovery Programs

They develop programs for recycling and recovering materials from the volume off switch and other components at the end of the device's life, promoting sustainability and reducing the need for new raw materials.
