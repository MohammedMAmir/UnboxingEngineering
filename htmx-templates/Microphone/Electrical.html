Microphone Circuit Design

Electrical engineers design the circuits that connect the microphone to the iPhone's processing unit, ensuring efficient and accurate signal conversion from analog to digital. This includes optimizing the microphone's power consumption and signal-to-noise ratio.

Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) Shielding

They also focus on EMI shielding for the microphone circuitry, preventing external electromagnetic fields from interfering with the audio signal, ensuring clean and clear sound capture.
