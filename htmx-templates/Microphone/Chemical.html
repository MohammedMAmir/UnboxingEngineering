Material Development for Diaphragm

Chemical engineers develop materials for the microphone's diaphragm that are lightweight and responsive to a wide range of sound frequencies. These materials must also be durable and stable under different environmental conditions.

Coatings for Moisture and Dust Resistance

They also work on protective coatings for the microphone components that resist moisture, dust, and other contaminants, ensuring long-term reliability and performance of the microphone in various environments.
