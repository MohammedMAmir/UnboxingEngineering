Coil Design for Optimal Energy Transfer

Electrical engineers design the wireless charging coil with a focus on maximizing energy transfer efficiency. They optimize the coil's shape, size, and number of turns to achieve the best possible inductive coupling with charging stations.

Circuit Integration for Safe Charging

They also design the circuits that connect the wireless charging coil to the battery, incorporating safety features such as overvoltage protection, temperature monitoring, and foreign object detection to ensure safe and reliable charging.
