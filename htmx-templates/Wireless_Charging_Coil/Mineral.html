Sustainable Sourcing of Coil Materials

Mineral engineers ensure that the materials used in the wireless charging coil, such as copper and other conductive metals, are sourced sustainably. They assess and optimize the supply chain for ethical sourcing and minimal environmental impact.

Refinement Processes for High Conductivity

They develop refinement processes that enhance the conductivity and purity of the metals used in the coil, ensuring optimal performance of the wireless charging system while adhering to environmental and ethical standards.
