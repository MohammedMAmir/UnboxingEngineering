Mechanical Design for Durability and Precision

Mechanical engineers design the SIM holder to be durable and precise, ensuring that it can withstand repeated use without failing or degrading in performance. They pay special attention to the mechanism for ejecting and securing the SIM holder within the device.

Thermal and Mechanical Stress Analysis

They conduct thermal and mechanical stress analysis to ensure that the SIM holder's design and materials can withstand the environmental conditions and handling that iPhones typically experience, ensuring long-term reliability.
