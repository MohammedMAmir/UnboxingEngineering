Materials for Corrosion Resistance

Chemical engineers develop materials for the SIM holder and its contacts that are resistant to corrosion, ensuring a long-lasting and reliable connection between the SIM card and the device. This includes selecting metals for the contacts that maintain good electrical conductivity over time.

Protective Coatings for Durability

They also work on protective coatings for the SIM holder that resist wear and tear from inserting and removing the SIM card. These coatings help maintain the functionality and appearance of the SIM holder throughout the life of the device.
