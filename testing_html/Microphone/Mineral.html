Responsible Sourcing of Raw Materials

Mineral engineers ensure that the raw materials used in the microphone, such as metals for the circuitry and magnets, are sourced responsibly. They work to minimize the environmental and social impact of mining activities associated with these materials.

Recycling and Material Recovery

They develop processes for recycling and recovering valuable materials from old or discarded devices, reducing the need for new raw materials and promoting a more sustainable lifecycle for electronic components.
