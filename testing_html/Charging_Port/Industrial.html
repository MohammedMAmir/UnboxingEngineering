Efficiency in Component Manufacturing

Industrial engineers optimize the manufacturing process for the charging port, ensuring high efficiency, yield, and quality. They develop production lines that can assemble charging ports with precision and at scale.

User-Centered Design and Ergonomics

Industrial engineers apply user-centered design principles and ergonomics to the development of the charging port, ensuring it is easy to use, accessible, and meets the users' needs for charging and data transfer. This includes considering the ease of plugging in and unplugging cables, the port's durability against repeated use, and its integration into the iPhone's overall design.
