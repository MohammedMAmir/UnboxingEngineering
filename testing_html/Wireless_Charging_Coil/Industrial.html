Streamlining Coil Production Processes

Industrial engineers streamline the production processes for the wireless charging coil, focusing on efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and scalability. They develop automated assembly lines that can precisely manufacture and integrate the coils into the iPhone with high consistency.

Quality Control and Testing Systems

They establish rigorous quality control and testing systems to ensure each wireless charging coil meets the required performance and safety standards. This includes testing for charging efficiency, durability, and compliance with international wireless charging standards.
