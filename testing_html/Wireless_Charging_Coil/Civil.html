Design of Eco-Friendly Production Facilities

Civil engineers design the production facilities where wireless charging coils and related components are manufactured, emphasizing eco-friendly and sustainable practices. These facilities are equipped with specialized machinery for precision manufacturing and assembly of the delicate coil components.

Infrastructure for Efficient Waste Management

In these facilities, civil engineers also develop efficient waste management systems to handle the byproducts of manufacturing processes, aiming to minimize environmental impact and recycle materials where possible.
