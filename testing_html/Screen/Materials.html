Advanced Materials for Screens

Materials scientists explore and develop new materials that enhance the performance and durability of the iPhone screen. They investigate properties like hardness, flexibility, and optical clarity to create materials that not only protect the screen from damage but also provide an exceptional visual experience.

Testing and Quality Assurance

Materials engineers also conduct extensive testing on screen materials under various conditions to ensure their longevity and reliability . This includes stress tests, impact resistance evaluations, and simulations of long-term use to predict how the materials will perform over the life of the device.
