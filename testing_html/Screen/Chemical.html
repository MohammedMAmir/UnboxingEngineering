Material Development

Chemical engineers work in formulating the materials that make up the smartphone screen, focusing on enhancing durability and functionality . They innovate in the creation of glass composites that are resistant to scratches and breaks , ensuring the screen remains functional and clear after everyday use. Additionally, they develop the conductive and transparent layers within the screen that enable its touch-sensitive capabilities, using materials like indium tin oxide to ensure precise touch responsiveness.

Contribution to Coatings

The development of surface treatments and coatings , such as anti-reflective or oleophobic coatings, is another critical area where chemical engineers contribute. These coatings are designed to reduce glare, repel water and oil, and minimize fingerprint marks, thereby enhancing the user experience.
