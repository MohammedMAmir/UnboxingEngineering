Sustainable Sourcing of Materials

Mineral engineers ensure that the materials used in the SIM holder, particularly metals for the contacts, are sourced in an environmentally responsible manner. They focus on minimizing the environmental impact of material extraction and processing.

Recycling Strategies for End-of-Life Devices

They develop strategies for recycling and recovering materials from SIM holders and other components when devices reach the end of their life, contributing to the sustainability of the manufacturing process and reducing the need for new raw materials.
