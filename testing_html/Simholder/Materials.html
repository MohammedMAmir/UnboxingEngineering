Advanced Materials for SIM Holder Construction

Materials scientists select advanced materials for the construction of the SIM holder that balance strength, flexibility, and weight, ensuring that the holder is durable yet lightweight. They often choose materials that complement the iPhone's overall design aesthetic.

Innovations in Electrical Contact Materials

They research and develop materials used for the electrical contacts in the SIM holder, focusing on materials that provide excellent electrical conductivity and resistance to wear and oxidation, ensuring a stable connection over time.
