Welcome to Unboxing Engineering!

  • Rotate: to rotate the model, left-click and drag on the screen.

  • Zooming: To inspect the model closer, you can zoom in with mousewheel up, and zoom back out with mousewheel down.

  • Inspection: To inspect one part in particular, left click on it.

  • Part Information: When you click on a part it's description will pop up on the right, as well as any included diagrams.Click the "X" button in the top right of the image popup to close it.

  • Zoomed Interaction: When you have selected a part, you can still interact with it in the left window

  • Disciplines: To learn about how different engineering disciplines contributed to the part you clicked on, click on the associated icon in the navbar

  • Returning to Main Model: Once you are done learning about a part, you can click on the "X" button on the top right of the description box to return to the main model
